Uvpressexpand-metasequoia-plugin

UVPressExpand is a plugin for Metasequoia 3D modelling tool. UVPressExpand adds a command to move multiple vertices simultaneously on UV view, acts like pushing and spreading a lump of cray out by a palm of your hand.
